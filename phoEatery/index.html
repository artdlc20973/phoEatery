    Phở or pho[a] is a Vietnamese soup dish consisting of broth, rice noodles (bánh phở), herbs, and meat (usually beef (phở bò), sometimes chicken (phở gà)). Phở is a popular food in Vietnam[5] where it is served in households, street stalls and restaurants countrywide. Residents of the city of Nam Định were the first to create Vietnamese traditional phở. Phở is considered Vietnam's national dish.
